About the Dataset

Data Source Overview

The job listings dataset is hosted on Kaggle (originally sourced from JobStreet Malaysia), covering a 12-month collection window.

Data Source
Kaggle (azraimohamad/jobstreet-all-job-dataset)
Collection Period
January 2024 – December 2024
Number of Records
~50,000+ unique job listings
Geographic Coverage
All 16 Malaysian states and territories
Dataset Attributes

Collected Data Fields

Eight structured attributes extracted from each job listing.

# Attribute Type Description
01 Job Title String The advertised role or position name
02 Company Name String Name of the hiring organisation
03 Industry Categorical Sector or industry classification
04 Location String State and city of the job posting
05 Salary Numeric Monthly salary range (MYR)
06 Skills Required Array List of required technical and soft skills
07 Employment Type Categorical Full-time, part-time, contract, freelance
08 Posted Date Date Date the listing was published on JobStreet
Platform Context

Why Choose JobStreet?

JobStreet (part of the SEEK family) is one of Southeast Asia's largest and most trusted online employment marketplaces. We selected it as the primary data source for InsightMY for several strategic reasons.

  • Market Dominance in Malaysia

    As the leading job portal in the country, it provides a highly representative sample of the Malaysian job market, covering both urban centers and rural states accurately.

  • Diverse Industry Coverage

    Job listings span from entry-level positions to executive roles across almost every conceivable sector, ensuring comprehensive and unbiased analytics.

  • Structured Data Quality

    Unlike unstructured job boards, JobStreet uses standardized fields for salary, location, and required skills, which makes data extraction and normalization significantly more reliable.

What is JobStreet?

Founded in 1997, JobStreet is a job portal that facilitates the matching and communication of job opportunities between jobseekers and employers. Serving millions of users across Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines, and Indonesia, its extensive database makes it an invaluable resource for true labor market analytics and career trend forecasting.

Project Background

Why This Project Matters

Malaysia's job market is evolving rapidly in response to digital transformation, post-pandemic economic recovery, and increasing foreign investment. Despite this, there is a significant lack of centralised, data-driven tools that help students, graduates, policymakers, and HR professionals understand the current state of employment demand.

InsightMY Analysis addresses this gap by leveraging big data techniques to collect, process, and visualise thousands of real job listings from JobStreet Malaysia — the country's most prominent employment platform — and present findings in an interactive, accessible dashboard.

Problem Statements

Identified Research Gaps

01
Fragmented Job Market Data
No centralised platform aggregates and visualises Malaysia's job market data in a structured, filterable manner accessible to non-technical users.
02
Skills-Gap Visibility
Educational institutions and job seekers lack quantified, evidence-based data on which skills Malaysian employers most urgently require.
03
Salary Opacity
Inconsistent salary disclosure in job postings makes it impossible for candidates to benchmark compensation expectations fairly across industries and regions.
